Why Logo?

Building, renovating or developing your dream home is a big decision, so why should you choose Logo? For more than four decades we have been building best places to live and we are here to show why.

  • Locally Owned & Operated

    Being family owned & operated means that everything we do, is done locally. We don't outsource any work or supplies overseas, meaning your dream home is built to the highest of standards.

  • Innovations in Buildings

    We build for the varied, dynamic and diverse needs of our clients. To do this, we are constantly creating agile home designs with a focus on innovative construction methods to suit all block types, budgets and lifestyles.
